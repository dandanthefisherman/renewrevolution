Toenail fungus is a common condition, and while over-the-counter treatments can assist, it may take a combination of approaches for noticeable improvement. Regular foot care can help in managing and preventing fungus growth effectively.

Here are some general tips on caring for toenail fungus:

  • Keep your feet clean and dry to minimize the conditions that allow fungus to thrive.
  • Consider using antifungal powders or sprays, particularly if your feet are prone to sweating.
  • Trim your nails regularly, ensuring they are neat to help reduce the risk of infections.
