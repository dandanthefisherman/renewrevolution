Toenail fungus is a common issue that can be difficult to manage. Over-the-counter treatments can be a helpful part of a plan to address the infection, but it may require a combination of steps for noticeable improvement.

Here are some tips on managing toenail fungus:

  • Keep your feet clean and dry to prevent the growth of fungi.
  • Use antifungal powders or sprays, especially if you're prone to sweating.
  • Trim nails regularly and avoid cutting too deep, which can lead to infections.
  • Look into specialized products that target toenail fungus.
